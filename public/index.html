About me

I'm a

Born in New Hampshire, USA, I have been playing guitar for 13 years and have been teaching for 11. I received my Bachelor's in Economics and minor in Music with a focus in jazz studies from the University of Rochester and The Eastman School of Music. Currently, I teach guitar students of all ages and musical interests. With classes broken up into two parts, theory and learning songs, I ensure that every student receives important knowledge not only about guitar, but music itself. By doing so I am able to tie in the practical applications of music theory to my student's favorite songs for a more in depth learning experience.

My Services

Guitar Teaching

With 11 years of guitar teaching experience, I have taught all ages, levels and styles of music. Each class has a focus on music theory and learning the student's favorite song. Expanding a guitarist's knowledge past basic shapes using theory is the key to guitar playing excellency and is a main focus in every class.

Music

With 17 years of total music experience I have worked on various independent projects musical projects including: writing, recording and performing. With an educational background in music theory and interests in a variety of different genres I bring a unique perspective and sound to any musical project.

Composition

Having written multiple compositions and full length songs in various genres, I have developed a unique approach to song writing. By working with a variety of different instruments and styles I ensure that my compositions continue to evolve as I pursue new unique and innovative sounds.
